Uttarakhand

Known as the "Land of Gods," Uttarakhand is famous for its snow-capped Himalayas, Kedarnath temple, Valley of Flowers, and adventure sports in Rishikesh. It is a perfect blend of spirituality and natural beauty.

Assam

Assam is the gateway to Northeast India, known for its lush tea gardens, Kaziranga National Park (home of the one-horned rhino), and the mighty Brahmaputra river. Its rich culture and wildlife attract tourists worldwide.

Maharashtra

Maharashtra is home to the bustling city of Mumbai, the Gateway of India, Ajanta-Ellora caves, and hill stations like Mahabaleshwar. It is a land of history, culture, and modern vibrancy.

Kerala

Popularly called "God’s Own Country," Kerala is known for its serene backwaters, houseboats, beaches, Kathakali dance, and Ayurveda. A perfect mix of culture and natural beauty.
