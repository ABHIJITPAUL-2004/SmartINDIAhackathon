Indian Artists

Raja Ravi Varma

Raja Ravi Varma was a renowned painter celebrated for blending Indian traditions with European art techniques, bringing mythological stories to life.

M. F. Husain

Known as the "Picasso of India," M. F. Husain was a modernist painter whose works explored Indian culture, mythology, and contemporary themes.

Rabindranath Tagore

Rabindranath Tagore, Nobel laureate, was not only a poet but also an accomplished painter, whose works captured deep emotions and spirituality.

Amrita Sher-Gil

Amrita Sher-Gil, often called India's Frida Kahlo, was a pioneer of modern Indian art, known for her expressive self-portraits and rural life depictions.
